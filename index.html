The bathrooms will make you feel like royalty, and no, that is not an exaggeration- these are not your typically Copenhagen bathrooms. Kick back and relax with a glass of wine in a gold trimmed, claw-footed bathtubs.

The Kitchens are built to be social. Simply put- they are huge. There is plenty of counter space for those who love to cook and there is a table to gather round. It is also completely equipped with everything a budding chef needs.

But how much does all that cost?

It’s very simple, you get one bill for all your fixed expenses like rent, utilities, cleaning services, internet & TV, laundry, toiletpaper, cleaning supplies, basic kitchen ingredients etc.

It even covers things you might not think about, like the cost of a big deposit, purchasing of furniture, wear and tear and of course the cost of painting and plaining of floors when you move out.

Single rooms starts from 8.500 pr month and double rooms from 11.000. The deposit is only 10.000 and you can move out with one month notice.

That is around 40% less than apartment rental - do your own calculations here

Help, I’m relocating to Copenhagen

We take care of you or your employees from start to finish; beside housing in a community of like-minded people, we take care of work permits and visas and offer a onboarding package to help you or your new employee settle in and get set up. Let us guide you new comers with a local perspective by people who truly love this city.
